## Quick Generation Tips

1. **Pick a persona** → determines stat ranges
2. **Select 6-13 sessions** → match persona focus areas
3. **Choose 9-28 connections** → 60% vendor, 30% peer, 10% speaker
4. **Add 2-4 achievements** → match engagement level
5. **Include 2-4 CTAs** → high priority sponsors + persona-relevant
6. **Select products** → match companies visited
7. **Use real company names** throughout
8. **Professional B2B tone** in all content
